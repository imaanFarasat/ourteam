Reza Piroznia – Founder & Owner

Reza Piroznia is the heart and soul of Reza Gem Collection. As a passionate gemologist and expert buyer, his main focus is sourcing the finest gemstones, beads, and materials from around the world. With a deep understanding of what jewelry makers need, Reza brings high-quality, hand-selected pieces directly to Canada for designers and artisans.
